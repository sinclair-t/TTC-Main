Account Manager

London, UK

Full-time

Job Overview

We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Account Manager to join our growing construction team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing client relationships, overseeing project communications, and ensuring customer satisfaction throughout the construction process.

Key Responsibilities

  • Serve as the primary point of contact for assigned client accounts
  • Coordinate project timelines and deliverables with internal teams
  • Maintain regular communication with clients regarding project progress
  • Identify opportunities for additional services and project expansions
  • Resolve client concerns and ensure high levels of customer satisfaction
  • Prepare and present project proposals and cost estimates
  • Collaborate with project managers and construction teams
  • Maintain accurate project documentation and client records

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Construction Management, or related field
  • 3+ years of experience in account management or client relations
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Experience in construction or related industry preferred
  • Proficiency in CRM software and Microsoft Office Suite
  • Excellent organizational and time management abilities
  • Valid driver's license and reliable transportation

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with performance-based bonuses
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company matching
  • Paid time off and holidays
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment
  • Opportunity for career advancement
